FAQ

Do you remove furniture?

Yes. Couches, chairs, dressers, tables, mattresses, box springs, rugs, and bulky household items.

Do you handle stairs?

Yes, but stairs can affect pricing because they add time and labor. Mention stairs in the quote form.

Are photos required?

No, but photos help us quote faster and more accurately. A wide photo and an access photo are best.

What do you not take?

We do not take hazardous materials, chemicals, paint, oil, gasoline, asbestos, medical waste, explosives, firearms, ammunition, biohazards, or illegal materials.
