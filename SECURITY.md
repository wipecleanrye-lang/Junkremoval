Security & Privacy

At Clean Up Junk Rye, protecting our customers' information is a priority. When you request a quote, upload photos, or contact us through our website, your information is handled securely and used only to provide the services you request.

We respect your privacy and never sell, rent, or share your personal information with outside parties for marketing purposes.

Any photos submitted through our quote form are used solely for estimating and completing services. Customer information, including names, phone numbers, addresses, and uploaded images, is kept confidential and accessible only to authorized members of our team.

Our website uses secure connections and industry-standard security practices to help protect your information during transmission and storage.

By using our website, you trust us with your information, and we are committed to maintaining that trust through responsible data handling and professional business practices.

If you have any questions regarding privacy or security, please contact us directly through our website or by phone.
