Customer Protection

Security, privacy, and service terms.

This page explains how we handle quote requests, uploaded photos, customer information, payments, service expectations, and job safety.

Your Information

When you request a quote, we may collect your name, phone number, town, job details, requested timing, and photos you choose to upload.

We use this information only to review your quote request, contact you about the job, schedule service, and keep accurate business records.

Photo Privacy

Uploaded photos are used to understand the job size, access, item type, and removal details. Photos are not sold or shared for marketing.

We do not publicly post customer addresses or private personal information without permission.

No Selling Customer Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade customer information to outside companies for advertising or marketing.

Customer information is used only for business communication, quote review, scheduling, payment tracking, and service records.

Quote Accuracy

Quotes are based on the information provided, including photos, descriptions, volume, weight, stairs, access, location, and disposal needs.

Final pricing may change if the job is larger, heavier, harder to access, or different from what was originally described.

Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for accurately identifying what should be removed and what should stay.

  • Remove valuables before service.
  • Secure pets before arrival.
  • Tell us about stairs, long carries, or heavy items.
  • Tell us about fragile areas or property concerns.

Items We Do Not Take

We do not remove hazardous, unsafe, illegal, or restricted materials unless specifically approved in writing.

  • Chemicals, oil, gasoline, or paint
  • Asbestos or medical waste
  • Explosives, firearms, or ammunition
  • Biohazards or illegal materials

Property Protection

We make reasonable efforts to protect customer property while removing junk. Customers should identify fragile items, hidden hazards, weak surfaces, or areas requiring extra care before work begins.

Pre-existing damage, hidden defects, or unsafe property conditions may limit responsibility.

Payment Terms

Payment is generally due when the job is completed unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Accepted payment methods may include Zelle, Venmo, cash, checks, or other approved methods.

Cancellations & Scheduling

Jobs may be rescheduled due to weather, unsafe conditions, vehicle issues, disposal site closures, emergencies, or schedule conflicts.

For some covered work, customers may have cancellation rights required by law. Any legally required cancellation rights will be honored.

Website Security

Our website uses secure connections when available, and quote information is handled through trusted business systems.

No online system is perfect, but we take reasonable steps to keep quote requests and customer information organized and protected.

Questions before submitting?

Call us if you are unsure about pricing, items we accept, photos, access, or scheduling.
