Customer Protection

Privacy, photos, payment, and service terms.

This page explains how we handle quote requests, uploaded photos, customer information, payments, job expectations, and safety.

Your Information

When you request a quote, we may collect your name, phone number, town, job details, requested timing, and photos you choose to upload. We use this information to review your request, contact you, schedule service, and keep business records.

Photo Privacy

Uploaded photos are used to understand job size, item type, access, and removal details. We do not sell customer photos or publicly post private customer information without permission.

No Selling Customer Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade customer information to outside companies for advertising or marketing.

Quote Accuracy

Quotes are based on the details provided. Final pricing may change if the job is larger, heavier, harder to access, or different from the original description.

Customer Responsibilities
  • Remove valuables before service.
  • Secure pets before arrival.
  • Identify items that should not be removed.
  • Tell us about stairs, long carries, fragile areas, or heavy items.
Items We Do Not Take

We do not remove hazardous, unsafe, illegal, or restricted materials including chemicals, oil, gasoline, paint, asbestos, medical waste, explosives, firearms, ammunition, biohazards, human or animal waste, or illegal materials.

Payment

Payment is generally due when the job is completed unless otherwise agreed. Accepted payment methods may include Zelle, Venmo, cash, checks, or another approved method.

Scheduling & Cancellations

Jobs may be rescheduled due to weather, unsafe conditions, vehicle issues, disposal site closures, emergencies, or schedule conflicts. Any cancellation rights required by law will be honored.

Questions before submitting?

Call if you are unsure about pricing, photos, accepted items, or access.
